Biography

Peng Zhang received the B.Eng. with first-class honors degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2011 (top 5%). He received the Ph.D. degree in the Electrical and Electronic Engineering Department at Imperial College London, UK, in 2016 (fully funded scholarship from A*STAR). He is currently a Research Scientist with the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), Singapore. His research interests include sparse signal processing, random matrix theory, lattice and scheduling algorithms.

Dr. Zhang is a recipient of the A*STAR Graduate Scholarship (Overseas) from the Agency for Science, Technology, and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. CV is available here.
